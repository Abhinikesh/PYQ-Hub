Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how PYQ Hub collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

When you register or use PYQ Hub, we may collect:

  • Account information such as your name, email address, and password (stored securely via Firebase Authentication).
  • Upload metadata including college, course, subject, year, and file details.
  • Usage data such as searches, downloads, and points earned.
  • Technical data including browser type, device information, and approximate location derived from IP address.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve the PYQ search, upload, and community features.
  • Authenticate users and maintain account security.
  • Calculate rewards, badges, and contribution statistics.
  • Respond to support requests and enforce our Terms of Use.

3. File Storage

Uploaded PYQ files are stored using third-party cloud services (including Cloudinary and Firebase). Files you upload may be visible to other authenticated users based on your sharing settings and platform functionality.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers that help us host, store, and operate the platform.
  • Law enforcement when required by applicable law or valid legal process.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser local storage to maintain your login session and preferences. You can clear this data through your browser settings, though you may need to sign in again.

6. Data Retention

We retain account and upload data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. You may request account deletion by contacting support.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures including encrypted connections and Firebase security rules. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us to make a request.

9. Children's Privacy

PYQ Hub is intended for students aged 16 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 16.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, email support.abc@gmail.com or visit our Support Center.
